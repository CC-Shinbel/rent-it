1. Introduction

Welcome to RentIt, a videoke rental platform operated by Certicode ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform to browse and rent videoke equipment.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register for an account on our platform
  • Place a rental order for videoke equipment
  • Contact our support team
  • Provide delivery address details

This information may include:

  • Full name and contact number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address within our service areas
  • Government-issued ID details (for verification at delivery)
  • Account credentials (username and password)

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our platform, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and usage patterns
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your videoke rental orders
  • Coordinate delivery and pickup of equipment to your location
  • Verify your identity upon delivery (ID requirement)
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Manage your account, booking history, and favorites
  • Calculate delivery fees based on your location
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information in the following limited situations:

  • Delivery Team: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with our delivery personnel to coordinate equipment drop-off and pickup
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who help us operate the platform (e.g., email/SMS notification services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of RentIt, Certicode, or our customers

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Password hashing and secure authentication
  • Session management and access controls
  • Regular security reviews of our platform
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173), you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: Know what personal data we collect and how it is used
  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data under certain conditions
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@rentit.ph.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Save your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Store your cart and favorites selections
  • Understand how you use our platform to improve it

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.
